Bamyan Province is world-famous for the huge and historic Buddhist monuments that were once located there. These statues were inscribed on UNESCO's List of Cultural Heritage in Danger in 2003. Archaeological research in Bamyan has been ongoing since the 1920s. In 2003, a UNESCO-supervised rescue campaign was organized to protect the remains of the Buddha and other sites in Bamyan.
