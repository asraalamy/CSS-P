Herat Province is one of the 34 provinces of Afghanistan, with the city of Herat as its capital. Herat is the second largest and second most populous province of Afghanistan. Its area is 55, 868 square kilometers and its population is 2,152,892. It is bordered by Turkmenistan to the north, Ghor and Badghis to the east, Farah to the south, and Iran to the west.
