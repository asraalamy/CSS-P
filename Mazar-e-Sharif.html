Mazar-e-Sharif Province, with its capital in Mazar-e-Sharif, is located in northern Afghanistan Mazar-e-Sharif is the fourth largest city in Afghanistan and is also known as Balkh. Its population was about 693,000 in the 2015 census. Balkh Province has an important strategic location on trade routes from the Far East to the Mediterranean and Europe.
